PuppyWallet

PuppyWallet is designed to be a cryptowallet software as a service. This idea was produced due to current trends in cryptocurrencies. It was developed with React.js, Firebase, and CoinGecko API. I implemented login authentification and form validation. Photoshop was used for the pink wallet image and images are open source images from Pixabay.com. Figma was used to organized UI/UX design as well.
